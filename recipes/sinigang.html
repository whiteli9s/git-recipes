Pork Sinigang Recipe


Easy steps on how to cook Pork Sinigang

With its very affordable ingredients... your family will surely love this very savory, tangy, delicious food!

Ingredients:

  • 1⁄2kg pork lion (chopped to small pieces)
  • 41⁄2 cup water
  • Tamarind/sinigang soup mix (22g)
  • 1-2 small tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 green chilli
  • 1 red onion (chopped)
  • 2-3pcs garlic gloves (minced)
  • 1 bunch of water spinach leaves
  • half of white raddish (sliced)
  • A quarter cup of string beans (sliced to small pieces)
  • Eggplant (sliced)
  • fish sauce
  • salt and pepper

Optional ingredient:

  • Okra

Steps:

  1. On medium high heat, pour some oil on a caserole. When heated enough, sauté onion and garlic. After a few minutes, add the chopped tomatoes. Cook until softened.
  2. Add the pork and sauté with onion, garlic and tomatoes until slightly brown.
  3. Pour 41⁄2 cup of water. And boil.
  4. When boiling, slowly pour tamarind/sinigang soup mix while mixing. Add green chilli.
  5. After a minute, turn down the heat, cover and simmer for 8-10 minutes. (Check the caserole occasionally. It will overflow).
  6. Add white raddish (and okra for the optional ingredient). Add fish sauce and salt and pepper to taste. Cover and let it simmer for 13-15 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  7. Add the egglant and string beans. Check if pork is tender. Simmer for another 10mins. Stir occasionally.
  8. Lastly, add water spinach. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed. Turn off the heat. Then serve.
