Crispy Kakiage Recipe

Taste of Japan. Savory, crispy Kakiage.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Carrots (thinly sliced; julienne)
  • 1 White Onion (thinly sliced)
  • 1 cup AP flour
  • 1 tsp Baking soda
  • 2 cups of cold water
  • Salt and pepper
  • Light soy sauce
  • Lemon
  • Sugar

Steps:

  1. On a bowl, mix 1 cup of AP flour, 1 tsp baking soda, and 2 cups of cold water. Stir thoroughly while pouring water slowly. Add salt and pepper. Set aside. (If you don't have cold water, you can add ice cubes to the batter.)
  2. On a seperate bowl, toss carrots and white onion with a little bit of flour.
  3. Remove excess flour from the vegetables before adding it to the batter. Mix the vegetables until coated.
  4. Heat enough amount of oil (use any kind of oil you want) on a deep frying pan until it reaches 177°Celsius (350°Fahrenheit).
  5. Fry the mixture up to 1-2 minutes or until crispy and puffy. Set aside on a wire rack to keep it crispy.
  6. For the dipping sauce, set a sauce pan on low heat, add 2 tblsp of soy sauce. Once slightly smoking, add half tsp of sugar. Mix until sugar melts. Turn of the fire before squeezing a dash of lemon. Taste the sauce and adjust flavor if needed.
  7. Serve and enjoy.
