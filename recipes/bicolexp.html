Bicol Express Recipe

A filipino classic dish. Spicy pork dish stewed in coconut milk.

Ingredients:

  • 1⁄2kg Pork loin (chopped into cubes)
  • 2 cups of fresh coconut milk
  • 1⁄4cup of water
  • 2 green chili (chopped)
  • 2-3 bird's eye chili (chopped)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 3 gloves gralic (minced)
  • 1⁄4cup shrimp paste
  • pepper

    • Steps:

    1. Set a pan on medium heat. Put some oil and heat until slightly smoking.
    2. Sauté onion until translucent then add garlic. After a minute, add the pork and green chili (save some of the green chili for toppings). Sauté until the pork is slightly brown. Add 1⁄4 cup of shrimp paste and sauté for another 1-2 minutes.
    3. Pour 2 cups of fresh coconut milk. Stir until slightly boiling. Then pour 1⁄4cup of water. Cover and let it boil.
    4. Once boiling, turn down the heat to simmer. Add bird's eye chili (save some for the toppings). Cover and simmer for 15-18minutes. Don't forget to check and stir occasionally.
    5. After 15-18 minutes, check the pork if tender. Cover and cook for another 15 minutes or until oil is slightly visible (the oil is actually from the coconut milk).
    6. Finally, before serving, add the remaining of the chilis on top. Enjoy.
